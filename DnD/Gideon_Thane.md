### Roleplaying Notes for Gideon Thane:

#### Core Concept

Gideon Thane is a man forged in the crucible of tragedy. Having reached Level 5, he is no longer just a victim of his haunted past; he has become a precision-engineered weapon of vengeance. His *Oath of Vengeance* defines his existence—he is the relentless hunter who closes the distance between himself and those who deserve judgment. Gideon is not a beacon of light; he is the shadow that strikes when the wicked least expect it.

#### Verbal Style

Gideon’s speech is sparse and functional. He does not waste words on pleasantries. His vocabulary is clinical and focused on combat efficiency. When he speaks of his mission, his tone becomes icy and intense. He does not debate with monsters; he delivers sentences. Because of his background as a *Haunted One*, he may have a tendency to mutter dark phrases or curses under his breath when he believes no one else is listening.

#### Mannerisms (Guidance)

* **The Clockwork Execution:** Use the *Clockwork Amulet* to represent Gideon’s obsessive desire for guaranteed results. During high-stakes encounters, roleplay him as checking his gear or calculating the exact timing of his strikes to ensure his *Divine Smite* finds its mark.
* **Tactical Focus:** Use *Vow of Enmity* to demonstrate his singularity of purpose. Do not narrate him fighting many foes at once; instead, show him focusing exclusively on the most dangerous target until it is incapacitated.
* **The Haunted Gaze:** When in social settings, he should appear distracted, eyes constantly scanning the room for threats, a reflection of his persistent trauma. He treats every room like a battlefield that he is currently scouting.
